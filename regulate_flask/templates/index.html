Why it matters

Designed for moments when people need a reset without leaving workflow.

The problem

  • Stress accumulates during fast-paced work.
  • People often skip breaks or push through overload.
  • There is rarely an immediate, structured way to reset.

The intervention

  • One touch starts a guided breathing cycle.
  • LED-style visuals reduce the need to think or count.
  • The experience is short, calming, and easy to repeat.
